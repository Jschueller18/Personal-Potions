Sodium-Rich Foods Reference

Understanding Sodium in Your Diet

Sodium is an essential electrolyte that helps maintain proper fluid balance and supports nerve and muscle function. However, excessive sodium intake can contribute to high blood pressure and other health issues in some individuals.

This reference guide lists common foods that are high in sodium, organized by food category. Understanding which foods contain significant amounts of sodium can help you make informed dietary choices based on your health goals and electrolyte needs.
